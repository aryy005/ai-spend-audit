# Metrics & Analytics

## North Star Metric
- **Total Annualized Savings Identified**: Cumulative sum of the monthly savings identified across all audits * 12. This proves the value of the tool to the market.

## Input Metrics (Leading Indicators)
1. **Landing Page Conversion Rate**: % of visitors who complete the spend input form. Target: >25% (since it's free and un-gated).
2. **Viral Coefficient (k-factor)**: Average number of new users generated by a single shareable `/audit/[slug]` URL.
3. **Lead Capture Rate**: % of users who provide their email *after* seeing the value. Target: >40%.
4. **Consultation CTA Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: % of users with >$500/mo savings who click the consultation CTA to book a call with Credex. Target: >5%.

## Behavioral Tracking (via PostHog)
- **Drop-off in Form**: Are people abandoning when asked for "Seats"? If so, make it default to 1.
- **Time to Value**: How fast from hitting the page to seeing the dollar amount saved? Target: <45 seconds.
- **"Ouch" Factor**: Time spent hovering over the red "You are overpaying" numbers.
