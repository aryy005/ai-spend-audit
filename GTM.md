# Go-To-Market Strategy (GTM)

## Target Audience
- Pre-seed to Series B Startup Founders.
- Engineering Managers / VP of Engineering.
- Fractional CTOs.

## Acquisition Channels
1. **Product Hunt Launch**: "Mint for AI Tool Spend". Emphasize the shareable receipt aspect.
2. **Twitter/X Build-in-Public**: Share anonymized aggregate stats. e.g., "70% of startups are paying for ChatGPT Team when their usage dictates Plus."
3. **Engineering Newsletters**: Sponsor or organically post in newsletters like ByteByteGo, TLDR, and Hacker Newsletter.
4. **Viral Loop**: The generated `/audit/[slug]` URL encourages founders to share their "score" or savings on Twitter and LinkedIn.

## Lifecycle & Retention
- **Immediate**: Deliver instant value on the Results Page.
- **Lead Capture**: Collect email via the CTA after value is delivered.
- **Nurture**: Monthly email update on new AI tools and pricing changes.
- **Conversion**: Offer Credex premium consultation services for teams with >$500/mo savings.
