# User Interviews

## Interview 1: Alex, CTO at a Series A FinTech (35 employees)
- **Current Spend**: ~$1,200/mo
- **Stack**: GitHub Copilot Business for all devs, ChatGPT Team for product/marketing, OpenAI API for internal tools.
- **Biggest Pain Point**: Visibility. "I approve the Expensify reports, but I have no idea if the marketing team is actually using their ChatGPT Team seats or if they logged in once."
- **Reaction to Audit**: "Wait, we're paying for Copilot Business but 4 of my frontend guys switched to Cursor on their personal cards last month and expensed it? We're double-paying."
- **Feedback on UI**: Loved the instant number. Requested a way to upload a CSV of expenses rather than manually typing tools.

## Interview 2: Sarah, Solo Bootstrapped Founder
- **Current Spend**: $60/mo
- **Stack**: ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro, Midjourney.
- **Biggest Pain Point**: Subscription fatigue. "I use Claude for coding and ChatGPT for copy, but I feel guilty paying for both."
- **Reaction to Audit**: The audit correctly pointed out that using the Anthropic API directly via a UI like TypingMind for her occasional coding tasks would drop her Claude bill from $20 to ~$3. 
- **Feedback on UI**: "The 'You are overpaying' headline made my stomach drop, which made me instantly pay attention. Very effective."

## Interview 3: David, VP Eng at an AI-wrapper startup (15 employees)
- **Current Spend**: $2,500/mo (heavy API usage + seats)
- **Stack**: Cursor Enterprise, Anthropic API, OpenAI API.
- **Biggest Pain Point**: "We defaulted to Enterprise tiers because we thought we needed the security, but we don't even use the SSO features."
- **Reaction to Audit**: The engine flagged the Cursor Enterprise seats ($40/user) as overkill for a 15-person team without SSO mandates, suggesting Pro ($20/user). Identified $300/mo in savings immediately.
- **Action Taken**: He actually downgraded their Cursor plan during the call.
