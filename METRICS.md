# Metrics & Analytics

## North Star Metric
- **Total Dollars Saved Identified**: Cumulative sum of the monthly savings identified across all audits.

## Input Metrics (Leading Indicators)
1. **Landing Page Conversion Rate**: % of visitors who complete the spend input form.
2. **Viral Coefficient**: Average number of new users generated by a single shareable `/audit/[slug]` URL.
3. **Lead Capture Rate**: % of users who provide their email to receive the report.
4. **Consultation CTA Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: % of users with >$500/mo savings who click the consultation CTA.

## Tech Stack Tracking
- PostHog for product analytics (funnel tracking, button clicks).
- Vercel Web Vitals for performance monitoring.
