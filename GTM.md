# Go-To-Market Strategy (GTM)

## The Core Premise: "Engineering as Marketing"
The AI Spend Audit isn't just a utility; it's a wedge product for Credex's core B2B SaaS negotiation business. By giving founders an instant, undeniable "Ouch" moment (seeing their wasted capital), we establish immediate authority and trust. We are productizing the first 30 minutes of a consulting call.

## Target Audience & Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- **Primary ICP**: Series A/B Startup Founders and VP Engineering / CTOs.
- **Why**: They are scaling fast, recently raised capital, and their team size (20-100) means software sprawl is happening rapidly. They have enough spend (~$2k-$5k/mo on AI tools) to make optimization worthwhile, but lack dedicated procurement teams.
- **Secondary**: Bootstrapped founders (high sensitivity to $50/mo waste). Good for virality, but not the primary monetization target.

## Acquisition Channels
1. **The "Receipt" Viral Loop (Primary Driver)**
   - **Mechanic**: The `/audit/[slug]` URL is designed specifically to be screenshotted and shared on Twitter/LinkedIn.
   - **Psychology**: Founders love demonstrating efficiency and "hustle". Sharing "I just saved $600/mo on AI tools by cutting redundant Copilot seats" signals operational rigor to their investors and peers.
2. **Community Infiltration (IndieHackers, YC Bookface, Slack Communities)**
   - We will not post "Check out our tool." We will post high-value tear-downs: *"We analyzed 100 startup AI stacks. 73% are paying for Claude Team when they only need individual Pro. Here is the math."* The CTA is the free audit link.
3. **Targeted Cold Emailing (Outbound)**
   - Target: Operations Managers or Fractional CFOs at Series A startups.
   - Message: "Are you paying for GitHub Copilot AND Cursor? If your team is >10 people, you're likely losing $200+/mo in redundant seats. Run this free 30-second audit."

## Lifecycle & Retention
- **Immediate Value**: The instant they hit 'Generate', they get a dollar figure of waste.
- **Lead Capture Hook**: We do not gate the results. We gate the *future value*. "AI pricing drops every month. Give us your email, and we'll ping you the day a cheaper alternative for your specific stack launches."
- **Monetization (Credex CTA)**: If savings >$500/mo, we surface a calendar link. "You're bleeding capital. Let our team negotiate your enterprise agreements and consolidate your billing. Book a 15-min tear-down."
